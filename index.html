Our Story

Opened in September 2019, MIMI Caffe was born from the need for a truly family-friendly space in Plzeň. We missed a place where parents could enjoy a high-quality cappuccino while their children played safely nearby.

Located in the historic House of Fairy Tales building near the Zoo entrance, we offer a complete family facility: changing tables, a spacious play corner, and absolutely no judgment if your kids ride tricycles between tables.
